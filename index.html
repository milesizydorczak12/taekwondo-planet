Come kick at Taekwondo Planet! With four dojangs in the greater Boston area, we offer the best mentorship for beginners to black belts. Our comprehensive training plans teach ITF (International Taekwondo Federation) style forms, sparring techniques, self-defense skills, as well as the cultural elements of the sport. We actually have perfected the art of the kamehameha from dragon ball and can teach this skill for an additional charge.
